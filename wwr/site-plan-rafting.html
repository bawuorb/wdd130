Overview

Purpose

Our main goal is in establishing an online presence for the rafting business to expand our reach, increase bookings, build trust with potential customers, provide valuable information, streamline operations, enhance brand recognition, and ultimately grow our business in a competitive market.

Audience

Scenario Questions and Answers:

  • Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on).

    • Our target customers are typically adults aged 18 and above who enjoy outdoor adventures and seek adrenaline-pumping experiences. They often have a moderate to comfortable financial situation, allowing them to invest in recreational activities like rafting. They are interested in outdoor sports, nature conservation, and sustainable tourism practices.

  • What do they want?

    • They want to experience the thrill of whitewater rafting in safe and picturesque environments. They seek memorable experiences that challenge them physically and mentally, while also offering opportunities for relaxation and connection with nature.

  • What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

    • When accessing the website, they expect to find detailed information about rafting trips offered, including difficulty levels, trip durations, safety measures, and equipment provided. They want to see photos and videos showcasing the rivers, rapids, and scenery to get a feel for the experience. They also expect clear pricing, booking options, and customer reviews/testimonials to gauge the quality and safety of the trips.
