History

The Awuor Company was founded in 2022 by adventure enthusiasts Ace and Arloh. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most reputable rafting companies in the region.

Over the years, the company has expanded its offerings to include a variety of rafting trips for all skill levels. From serene river floats to adrenaline-pumping whitewater adventures, Awuor Company caters to everyone looking to experience the thrill of rafting.

With a commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and providing unforgettable experiences, the Awuor Company continues to be a leader in the rafting community.
